Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Hydra Trainer (hereinafter "App") handles personal data. The App is a collection of cognitive exercise and test modules (e.g., equations, adaptive matrix test, continuous addition test, anagrams, number sequences) and works entirely locally on your device, without accounts, cloud sync, or advertising.

1. Data Controller

Johannes Kropatschek
Vienna, Austria
Email: office@hydrapt.com

2. Scope and Purpose

This policy applies to the use of the App on iOS/iPadOS. The App provides cognitive training and self-assessment features for informational and entertainment purposes.

3. What Data Does the App Process?

The App does not transmit any personal data to the developer or third parties.

All information mentioned below remains exclusively on your device.

  • Usage and performance data (on-device): Test progress, answers, scores, timer states, local result overviews.
  • App settings (on-device): e.g., light/dark mode, UI preferences.
  • No special categories: No health or biometric data in the legal sense is collected or transmitted.
  • No identifiers/tracking: There are no accounts, no cross-device recognition, no advertising IDs, no analytics SDKs.
  • No network connections: The App does not establish its own server connections for operation. Operating system services (e.g., App Store updates, optional device diagnostic sharing with Apple) are outside the developer's control.

When you install Hydra Trainer from the App Store, Apple collects and aggregates telemetry data and provides it to us. This information includes the number of people who downloaded the app, the number of people who used it on a given day, and the total number of users per country. Apple does not share information about specific users with us.

4. Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Provision of the App (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing of the above-mentioned on-device data is necessary to provide the app features (tests, results, settings).
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): General app security and stability on your device. However, no server-side processing by the developer takes place.

5. Retention and Deletion

  • On the device: Data remains stored until you reset it in the app (if a reset function is available) or uninstall the app.
  • With the developer: No personal data is stored.

6. Recipients and Disclosure

No disclosure. No personal data is transmitted to third parties.

No international data transfers.

7. App Permissions

The App does not use any sensitive device permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, photos, location, or HealthKit. Should this change in the future, you will be asked for consent before use, and this policy will be updated.

8. Your Rights (Art. 12-23 GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection.

Since data is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to the developer, these rights can practically be exercised by you yourself (reset function or uninstallation). For questions, contact office@hydrapt.com.

9. Minors

The App is not specifically aimed at children. No tracking or collection of personal data by the developer takes place.

10. Data Security

Data processing takes place exclusively on your device and is subject to the security mechanisms of iOS/iPadOS. Please protect your device (e.g., device passcode, Face/Touch ID).

11. External Services

The App does not integrate any external analytics services, ad networks, or social plugins. Any operating system functions (e.g., system-wide crash reports to Apple, if enabled by you) are Apple's responsibility.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if features or legal requirements change. The current version is always available in the app or via the linked notice. See date above.

13. Contact & Right to Complain

Privacy questions: office@hydrapt.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. In Austria: Austrian Data Protection Authority (dsb.gv.at).

Disclaimer (Not a Medical Device)

The App serves training, information, and entertainment purposes. It is not a medical or psychological diagnostic or therapeutic tool and does not replace professional advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
