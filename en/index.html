FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

The most important answers about Hydra Psychology Trainer.

Who is Hydra Psychology Trainer designed for?

The app is designed for applicants to police, military, and special forces units, as well as anyone who needs to complete a psychological aptitude assessment, such as senior and commanding officers. The tests offered are also used in admission procedures for various fields of study, such as medicine and psychology.

Is the app free?

The download is free. You can test selected modules for 24 hours. For full access, there is a lifetime license via in-app purchase (one-time payment of EUR 19.99, no subscription).

What test areas are covered?

Training covers logic & patterns, arithmetic, language comprehension, perception & attention, and other formats similar to those found in the Vienna Test System. The Vienna Test System is one of the internationally established and widely used computer-based test systems.

How is my data stored?

All data stays locally on your device. There are no accounts, no tracking, no cloud synchronization, and no advertising.

Do I need an internet connection?

No internet connection is required for training in the app. You can train on a plane or abroad without a data plan. System services such as App Store updates are outside the app's scope.
